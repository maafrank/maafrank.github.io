Boardsports

Surfing, Snowboarding & Skateboarding

Riding the Elements

Board sports have been a huge part of my life - from catching waves in the ocean to carving down snow-covered mountains and cruising through city streets. Each discipline offers its own unique thrill and challenge.

Surfing

Connects me with the ocean's raw power and rhythm. There's nothing quite like paddling out early in the morning and catching that perfect wave. I load up the 4Runner with my board and head to the coast whenever the swell is good.

Snowboarding

Takes me to the mountains where I can experience the rush of speed and the beauty of winter landscapes. The feeling of floating through powder is unmatched. Whether it's a day trip or a camping adventure in the snow, snowboarding never gets old.

Skateboarding

Keeps me connected to my roots and provides a creative outlet for expression and technical progression. It's the most accessible of my board sports - just grab the board and go.

Camping & Outdoor Adventures

Car camping is my preferred way to disconnect and recharge. The 4Runner gets loaded up with gear, and I head out to explore California's incredible landscapes - from coastal campgrounds to mountain hideaways to snow-covered wilderness areas. There's something therapeutic about setting up camp, cooking over a fire, and sleeping under the stars.

I love the versatility of car camping - whether it's a beach camping trip with the surfboard, a fishing expedition to a remote lake, or a winter camping adventure near the slopes, the 4Runner handles it all. Airing down the tires for sand and snow, setting up camp, and being fully self-sufficient in the backcountry is incredibly rewarding.

Fishing & Fly Fishing

Fishing is the perfect complement to my camping trips. Whether I'm casting a fly line in a mountain stream or fishing from shore at a remote lake, the patience and focus required provide a welcome contrast to the fast-paced world of tech. There's a unique satisfaction in reading the water, selecting the right approach, and connecting with nature.

Fly fishing especially appeals to my analytical side - it's part art, part science. Understanding insect hatches, water conditions, and fish behavior makes each outing a learning experience. Some of my best camping trips have been to remote fishing spots where I can cast all day and camp by the water at night.

Travel fuels my curiosity and broadens my perspective.
